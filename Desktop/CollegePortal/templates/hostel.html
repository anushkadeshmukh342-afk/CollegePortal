Application Process
Required Documents:
  • Completed hostel application form
  • College admission receipt
  • Income certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Character certificate from previous institution
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Address proof and ID proof
Application Steps:
  1. Download application form from college website
  2. Fill the form completely and accurately
  3. Attach all required documents
  4. Submit to hostel office with application fee
  5. Wait for allocation based on availability
  6. Pay hostel fees upon room allocation
  7. Complete check-in process with warden
Note: Hostel admission is subject to availability. Priority given to outstation students.
Rules and Regulations
General Rules:
  • Students must return to hostel by 9:00 PM
  • Visitors allowed only during specified hours
  • No smoking, drinking, or substance abuse
  • Maintain cleanliness in rooms and common areas
  • Respect hostel property and fellow residents
  • Report any maintenance issues immediately
Disciplinary Actions:
  • Warning for minor violations
  • Fine for property damage
  • Suspension from hostel for serious violations
  • Expulsion for repeated offenses
  • No refund of fees in case of expulsion
  • Parents will be informed of any violations
Important: Violation of rules may lead to immediate termination of hostel accommodation.
